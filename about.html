I have a Few Other Passions That I Enjoy In My Spare Time

Magic The Gathering

I started collecting magic cards at an early age. Not really understanding the cards or how to play the game. I just enjoyed looking at the art and collecting. Later on in middle school I learned how to play a format called "Modern" and started playing casually with friends.

Magic is a game that requires a lot of skill and strategy to play. You can build your own decks or buy pre-made decks and learn to play those. I started getting more and more interested in Magic the Gathering in my early 20's where I stated playing a format called "Draft". This is a format where you open packs and pull one card at a time while you pass the packs around the table to all the players and make a deck out of the cards you pull. This restarted the fire of collecting for me.

My most recent fascination is with a format called "Commander" where you pick one Creature card to be your main focus of the deck and you build your deck around the strategy of that creature. It is a 100 card deck and singleton format which means you can only have one of each card in the deck. These games can be very competitive but I tend to play casual, which can make the game last much longer but I think tend to be more fun. I enjoy playing Magic with my partner, sister and brother in law on the weekends.

Live Action Role Play (LARPing)

LARP is a group activity where you get to create a character with skills and statistics like health, mental fortitude and other attributes. Then you get to create how you want to be perceived by make uo and costume. A lot goes into work from making costumes and foam weaponry to the physical aspect of running around and engaging in roleplay and combat.

I started LARPing when I lived in Indiana, I joined a group called Dystopia Rising which was a Zombie Apocalypse style game. I enjoy doing special fx make uo and designing costumes a lot. Dressing up and escaping from the real world for a day or two over the weekend with some friends while getting some fresh air is great stress relief.

Occult Studies

My love for all things dark and spooky such as Halloween, Special FX make up, Killer Clowns and Graveyards extends past that into the unknown such as Paranormal and Occult Studies. At a young age I was gifted a set of Tarot Cards from my mother which sparked a big flame within me to learn all about the mystic arts and the history behind it.

I have read several books by authors such as Scott Cunnigham and Anton LaVey. Studying different Polytheistic Religions and alternative belief systems. As well as paranormal phenomenons.
